All the Details

My interest in web design began early when I joined the “Geek Squad” in 5th grade, fixing computers for my teachers. Since then, I’ve explored technology through childhood vlogs and social media. While I haven’t fully experienced the professional design world, I’m excited for my career and where my path will lead. Outside of designing, I try to step away from screens to rest my eyes. I spend time working out, painting, cooking, or enjoying fresh air.

Softball has been a central part of my life, teaching me countless lessons. Over 15 years, I’ve faced adversity, team struggles, and personal doubts. These experiences taught me resilience, confidence in challenges, and the value of observation during growth. Most importantly, they instilled in me the importance of kindness and care toward others, because we never truly know the impact we can have on someone’s life.
